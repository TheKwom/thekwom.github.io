PARC: Alignment

Nintendo

nintendo.com

Here we see alignment in the name, release date, price and console for which the game is made. They are all left aligned perfectly, creating a strong line that is clearly noticable even though there isn't an actual line there.

PARC: Proximity

Best Buy

bestbuy.com

On this website we see the images of each product placed consistently to the left of their description, price and other information. Our mind notices the proximity and instantly realizes that those two things are related to one another.

PARC: Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Something I have always loved about the Apple website is their use of colors and contrast. In this particular screenshot we see that nothing is difficult to read. The things that are more inportant, such as the name of the product and how to purchase it, stand out far more because of their contrast.
